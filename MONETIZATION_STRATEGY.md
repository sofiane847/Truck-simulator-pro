# Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Streams
1. **In-App Purchases (IAP)**: Offer players options to buy in-game currency, skins, and enhancements.
2. **Advertisements**: Implement ad placements for free players, including rewarded video ads and standard ad placements.
3. **Premium Version**: Offer a one-time purchase option for an ad-free experience with additional features.
4. **Subscriptions**: Monthly or yearly subscriptions that offer perks like exclusive content, discounts, or premium items.

## KPIs to Track
- **Daily Active Users (DAU)**: Measure engagement by tracking the number of unique users daily.
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: Calculate the average revenue per player.
- **Churn Rate**: Measure the rate at which players stop playing.
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Estimate the total revenue expected from a player over time.

## A/B Testing Plan
1. **Test different pricing models**: Analyze which pricing structure yields better conversion rates.
2. **Ad placements vs. no ads**: Compare user engagement and revenue between ad-supported and ad-free experiences.
3. **Subscription perks**: Test different benefits provided in subscription packages to see which is more appealing.
